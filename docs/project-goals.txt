Goal
Configure a fresh Salesforce org to support a complete B2B sales motion for NovaTech Industrial Systems, including lead-to-cash processes, quoting, forecasting, and renewals. The org will simulate a real implementation with security, data model, and sales process customization aligned to business requirements.

Sales Scenario
NovaTech Industrial Systems is a B2B manufacturer and solutions provider in industrial automation.

Company Type
B2B industrial equipment + software-enabled services.

What is being sold
	Capital equipment (bulk/large deals): industrial sensors, controllers, and gateway hardware sold in bundles for plant deployments
	Subscriptions: “NovaTech Monitor” (cloud monitoring + analytics) licensed per site/device
	Add-ons: premium support tiers, installation/commissioning services, training packages, and extended warranties

Who sells it
	NovaTech Sales team (Sales Reps + Sales Managers) sells to mid-market and enterprise manufacturing customers
	RevOps supports process, reporting, and forecasting

What success looks like
	Increased subscription attach rate (equipment deals that include Monitor subscriptions)
	Higher renewal rates and expansion (adding sites/devices, upgrading support tiers)
	Accurate pipeline + forecast visibility across regions
